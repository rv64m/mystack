Re-reading *The Elements of Typographic Style* by Robert Bringhurst. Still the best book on typography after all these years.

> "Typography exists to honor content."

Every time I return to this book, I find something new. Today's insight: the importance of optical alignment over mathematical alignment. Sometimes things need to feel right more than they need to measure right.
