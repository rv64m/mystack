Project

Quiet Reader

A distraction-free reading app for long-form articles and ePub books.

Role Design & Development
Year 2025
Stack Swift, SwiftUI, Core Text
Status live

Quiet Reader is a reading app designed for focus. No social features. No sync conflicts. Just your books and articles, presented beautifully.

Problem

Most reading apps are cluttered with features you don’t need: social sharing, achievement badges, algorithmic recommendations. For people who just want to read, this noise is exhausting.

Solution

Quiet Reader strips away everything non-essential. The interface is deliberately minimal: a book cover, your reading position, and the text itself. Settings are limited to typography, spacing, and theme — the things that actually affect reading comfort.

Results

Since launching in late 2025, Quiet Reader has attracted a small but dedicated audience of readers who value focus over features.
